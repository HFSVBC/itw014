class="w3-container">

Single-Player

In Single-Player mode, the objective of the game is to guess a certain word, letter by letter ,in a space of time. For every wrong letter you lose one "life", and after a limited number of lives lost, the game ends. The greater the difficulty chosen, the lower the playing time.

Multi-Player

In Multi-Player mode, two players choose one word each for the opponent to guess. After the choice, both players play side by side at the same time, with the same rules of the Single-Player mode.
